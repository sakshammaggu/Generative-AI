## **🟢 1. Beginner Level: Fundamentals of AI & Deep Learning**  
To master Generative AI, you must first understand AI, ML, and deep learning.

### **1️⃣ Mathematics for AI**
✅ Linear Algebra:
- Scalars, Vectors, Matrices, Tensors  
- Matrix Operations (Addition, Multiplication, Inverse, Determinants)  
- Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)  

✅ Probability & Statistics:
- Probability Distributions (Normal, Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson)  
- Conditional Probability, Bayes Theorem  
- Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)  

✅ Calculus:
- Differentiation & Integration  
- Partial Derivatives & Chain Rule  
- Gradient Descent & Optimization  

✅ Optimization Techniques:
- Convex vs. Non-Convex Optimization  
- Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD), Adam, RMSProp, Momentum  
- Lagrange Multipliers  

📚 **Resources:**  
- "Mathematics for Machine Learning" by Deisenroth et al.  
- 3Blue1Brown YouTube Series (Linear Algebra, Calculus)
